## Voice & tone

- [x] **Simple, not complex or academic.** Short sentences. Plain English. No jargon outside trademarked EOS terms.
- [x] **Helpful, not patronizing.** Copy assumes the reader is an experienced entrepreneur.
- [x] **Active voice throughout.**
- [x] **Positive framing.** "Get what you want from your business" — not "stop being frustrated."
- [x] **Humbly confident.** No arrogance. No comparisons to competitors. No positioning against other systems.
- [x] **Help-first.** The 90 Minute Meeting is genuinely free. No bait-and-switch.
- [x] **Abundance-minded.** No competitor mentions, no "EOS vs. X" content.
