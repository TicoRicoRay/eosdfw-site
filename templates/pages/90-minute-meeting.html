What we'll cover

Who should be in the room

Your entire leadership team. Not just the owner. EOS® is a team sport, and the 90 Minute Meeting is the first time your team will experience the way we work together. Five to seven people is typical. Two-hour blocks scheduled in advance work best.

The 90 Minute Meeting is genuinely free. There is no fee, no follow-up obligation, and no pressure. If we decide we're not a fit, you'll leave with a clearer picture of EOS® and a few practical tools your team can start using on Monday.

If we are a fit, the next step is Focus Day® — usually scheduled within 30 days.
