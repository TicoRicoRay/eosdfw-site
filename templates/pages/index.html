The EOS Model®

Strengthen the Six Key Components® of your business.

Implementing EOS® is a journey to strengthen the Six Key Components® of your business. We work through each one in a deliberate order, using a proven set of simple, practical tools.

  • →
    Vision Component®
    Clarify exactly what your business is, where it's going, and how it will get there.
  • →
    People Component®
    Get the right people in the right seats throughout the organization.
  • →
    Data Component®
    Run your business on a handful of metrics — not gut feel or hunches.
  • →
    Issues Component®
    Build the discipline to identify, discuss, and solve issues for the long-term greater good.
  • →
    Process Component®
    Document and systemize the core processes that drive your business.
  • →
    Traction Component®
    Execute. Bring discipline and accountability into the organization every day.
