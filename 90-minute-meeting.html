The 90 Minute Meeting.

Ninety minutes with your leadership team. I share an overview of EOS®, your team asks questions, and by the end you'll know whether EOS® is right for your business — and whether we're a fit to work together.

Schedule on my calendar Email me directly

What we'll cover

  • An overview of EOS®. The three things every leadership team wants: Vision, Traction®, and Healthy.
  • The Six Key Components®. A simple model your team can use to assess the strength of your business today.
  • How implementation actually works. The 10 days, two years, and what you'd commit to as a team.
  • Your questions. Whatever your leadership team is wondering — we'll talk about it.
  • A clear next step. Either we move forward together, or you leave with tools you can use either way.

Who should be in the room

Your entire leadership team. Not just the owner. EOS® is a team sport, and the 90 Minute Meeting is the first time your team will experience the way we work together. Five to seven people is typical. Two-hour blocks scheduled in advance work best.

The fine print — there isn't any

The 90 Minute Meeting is genuinely free. There is no fee, no follow-up obligation, and no pressure. If we decide we're not a fit, you'll leave with a clearer picture of EOS® and a few practical tools your team can start using on Monday.

If we are a fit, the next step is Focus Day® — usually scheduled within 30 days.

Two ways to schedule

The fastest way is to book directly on my calendar. Pick a time, fill in your team's details, and I'll confirm by email.

Prefer email first? Reach out at ray.myers@eosworldwide.com and we'll find a time that works for your whole team.

Ready to schedule?

Pick a time on my calendar. I'll confirm by email and send a short prep note for your leadership team.
